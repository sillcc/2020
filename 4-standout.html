2019

The stand is made out of 4 panels. The panels divide into 4 different types, that can be combined (empty, shelves, table, holes). The user is free to combine the elements as he or she wants, depending on the products that have to be displayed and on the available space.

Materials like polycarbonate sheets and aluminium profiles make the stand stable and durable, for both internal and external use; the slight transparency of the polycarbonate also helps to validate the aestethic qualities of the product.
